## Design Philosophy: Heritage & Scripture-Rooted Reverence

This redesign reflects **Pastor Stephen E. Broden's commitment to doctrinal clarity, expository preaching, and biblical authority**. The design avoids contemporary church marketing trends and instead emphasizes truth-first communication with a reverent, scholarly tone.

---

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage | Reasoning |
|-------|-----|-------|-----------|
| Deep Bronze | #6F4E37 | Primary accent, headings, CTAs | Heritage, stability, scriptural authority |
| Ivory / Light Sand | #EFE8DF | Background, soft accents | Warmth, approachability, light of Scripture |
| Black | #000000 | Body text, strong contrast | Authority, clarity, readability |
| White | #FFFFFF | Backgrounds, overlays | Purity, clarity, contrast |
| Warm Gray | #8B8680 | Secondary text, borders | Sophistication, visual hierarchy |

---

## Typography System

### Headings
- **Font**: Georgia or similar serif (classic, scholarly, reverent)
- **Weights**: Bold (700) for main headings, Regular (400) for subheadings
- **Purpose**: Establish authority and doctrinal weight

### Body Text
- **Font**: Inter or similar clean sans-serif
- **Weight**: Regular (400) for body, Medium (500) for emphasis
- **Purpose**: Readability, accessibility, modern clarity

### Tagline / Scripture
- **Font**: Georgia italic or serif italic
- **Purpose**: Distinguish Scripture and inspirational quotes

---

## Layout Paradigm

### Asymmetric, Content-First Structure
- **Hero Section**: Dual CTA approach (Visitor + Member) with clear visual hierarchy
- **Content Sections**: Alternating left-right layouts with generous whitespace
- **Navigation**: Minimal, doctrinal-focused top nav with clear escape routes
- **Footer**: Simple, reverent, with essential links only

### Key Principles
1. **Whitespace as Design**: Ample breathing room between sections
2. **Hierarchy Through Scale**: Large, bold headings for primary messages
3. **Visual Depth**: Subtle shadows and layering for sophistication
4. **Responsive Flexibility**: Mobile-first approach with thoughtful breakpoints

---

## Signature Design Elements

### 1. Cross + Star Motif
- Subtle cross icon in header/footer
- Star accent for "A Light of Hope" tagline
- Used sparingly to reinforce doctrinal identity

### 2. Scripture Callouts
- Italicized Scripture passages in bronze or dark gray
- Bordered or highlighted for emphasis
- Consistent styling across all pages

### 3. Reverent Imagery
- Church building silhouettes or architectural elements
- Hands in prayer or fellowship (warm, human, not stock-photo)
- Light/illumination metaphors (candles, sunrise, stained glass)
- Avoid overly emotional or entertainment-style imagery

---

## Interaction Philosophy

### Micro-Interactions
- **Hover States**: Subtle bronze underline or color shift on links
- **Button Interactions**: Smooth transitions, clear active states
- **Scroll Behavior**: Gentle fade-ins for content sections
- **Form Feedback**: Clear validation messages, no jarring errors

### Navigation
- **Top Navigation**: Fixed or sticky, minimal items (Home, About, Beliefs, Sermons, Visit, Contact)
- **CTAs**: Two primary CTAs on hero (Plan Your Visit, Watch Latest Sermon)
- **Escape Routes**: Clear back buttons, breadcrumbs where needed

---

## Animation Guidelines

### Entrance Animations
- **Fade-in**: Content sections fade in on scroll (subtle, 0.3s duration)
- **Slide-up**: Hero section elements slide up from bottom (0.5s, staggered)
- **Scale**: Icons or accent elements gently scale on hover (1.05x)

### Interaction Animations
- **Link Hover**: Bronze underline appears (0.2s ease-in-out)
- **Button Hover**: Background color shift, slight shadow increase (0.2s)
- **Form Focus**: Input border color changes to bronze (0.15s)

### Avoid
- Excessive animations that distract from content
- Auto-playing videos or sounds
- Parallax effects that feel gimmicky

## Implementation Notes

- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with breakpoints at 640px, 1024px, 1280px
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, clear focus states, semantic HTML
- **Performance**: Optimize images, lazy-load content, minimize animations
- **SEO**: Semantic HTML, clear meta descriptions, structured data for church schema
